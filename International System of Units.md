|  second  |  $\text{s}$  |           time            |
|  metre   |  $\text{m}$  |          length           |
| kilogram | $\text{kg}$  |           mass            |
|  ampere  |  $\text{A}$  |     electric current      |
|  kelvin  |  $\text{K}$  | thermodynamic temperature |
